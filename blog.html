Impact of Techonology

Technology is the use of scientific knowledge for practical purposes or applications, whether in industry or in our everyday lives. So, basically, whenever we use our scientific knowledge to achieve some specific purpose, we're using technology. Well, there is slightly more to it than that. Technology usually involves a specific piece of equipment, but that equipment can be incredibly simple or dazzlingly complex. It can be anything from the discovery of the wheel, all the way up to computers.The Internet is the Information Age's decisive technology, and with the explosion of wireless communication in the early twenty-first century, we can say that humanity is now almost entirely connected, albeit with great levels of bandwidth, efficiency and price inequality.
The early years of the 21st century saw an explosion in Internet usage. The popularity of this global interconnected network system continues to grow continuously from the day it appeared in human life. Obviously, with universal access to information and an interactive communication mechanism that the Internet offers, it is not difficult to understand why people around the world are addicted to it. E-Commerce, E-Business and E-Marketing are phenomena that appear as a result of the invasion of the Internet. There is no doubt about the benefits of using the Internet in daily life.
